# 00 — Brand Constitution
> Load this into every content generation. Never shorten it.

---

## Identity

- **Brand:** Velum (@thevelum.studio · thevelum.studio). Always "Velum" — never "The Velum" in copy, never "The BackRoom". The "the" exists only in the handle/domain.
- **Category:** Strategic operator studio for creator-led businesses. Not coaching, not an agency, not an AI tool brand.
- **Positioning:** The quiet strategist behind smart creators. Invisible business infrastructure. Faceless by design.
- **Taglines:** Primary — "Strategic infrastructure for the creator economy." Secondary — "The business behind the creator."
- **Hero line:** "You've built a real audience. Nobody taught you how to make a business out of it."
- **Footer standard:** `— Velum — @thevelum.studio —`

## ICP — the authority-led creator

One-line filter: *Has she built real audience trust through a consistent point of view — and does she have a commercial gap that structural thinking can close?*

- Women creators, 1K–50K followers, 12–24+ months building
- POV-driven content, not entertainment-first
- Audience and trust proven; monetisation attempted but never compounded
- Wants a business, not just income
- Niche core: slow living, interiors, food, home, wellness aesthetics. The aesthetic register is the taste signal, **not** the reach limit — copy operates at the abstraction level of all authority-led creators.

## Voice

Confident. Warm but never soft. Every word earns its place.

| Principle | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Direct without being cold | State the thing. Warmth comes from specificity, not hedging. |
| Precise without being clinical | Name the actual problem in concrete terms. The reader recognises herself without being told how to feel. |
| Editorial not promotional | No filler, no exclamation marks, no urgency. A considered point of view, not a pitch. |
| Authority without ego | Credibility comes from the document, the system, the reasoning. Never announced. |

**Sounds like:** a senior strategist who has solved this before · an operator who thinks in systems, speaks in outcomes · an editor who cuts what doesn't need to be there.

**Never sounds like:** a hype account · a coach · a tech company · a lifestyle brand · someone who needs you to like them.

## Vocabulary system

**Always use:** strategic sprint · offer architecture · monetisation roadmap · 30-day plan · commercial structure · business infrastructure · authority-led creator · point of view content · commercial gap · offer ladder · creator economy · The Velum Blueprint · audience trust · strategic partner *(public)* · strategic operator *(internal only)*

**Never use:** coaching programme · community · transformation · unlock · alignment · abundance · grow your brand · passion · journey · scale your influence · dream business · safe space · girlboss · manifest · AI-powered *(as headline or primary descriptor)* · programme/course *(for our offers)*

**Substitutions:**

| Instead of | Use |
|---|---|
| coaching programme | strategic sprint / structured engagement |
| lifestyle creator (as ICP label) | authority-led creator |
| grow your audience | build commercial structure under your audience |
| scale your business | build a monetisation system that compounds |
| help you | build / map / design / architect |
| content strategy | offer architecture / commercial structure |
| I'm passionate about... | [remove — let the work speak] |

## Banned patterns (hard fails — rewrite on sight)

1. **The negation formula.** "This isn't X. It's not Y. It's Z." Exhausted pattern, reads as AI-generated. *(Exception: Vera's locked launch script, approved before this rule — do not replicate the pattern in new copy.)*
2. Exclamation marks. Emojis. Urgency tactics ("limited spots", "don't miss out").
3. Transformation/coaching language of any kind.
4. Hedging and qualifiers ("just", "maybe", "I think", "kind of").
5. Italics for emphasis in body copy — the gold italic accent in headlines is the only emphasis device.
6. Founder imagery or personal-branding framing. Velum is faceless — presence through system, not personality.

## Visual system — v5 (LOCKED)

**Colours:** Clear `#F0EBE1` dominant ~70% · Navy `#1B2A4A` impact moments · Gold `#D4AF37` precise accent, max ~8%, never large fills · Slate `#6B6F80` secondary text · Photo Dark `#1F1C18` within photography only.

**Typography (social formats):**
- Headlines: **DM Serif Display 400** — all social formats
- Italic accent: DM Serif Display Italic, gold, **one per headline** — the word that shifts the meaning
- Eyebrow: DM Sans 700, uppercase, gold, preceded by a short gold dash
- Body/sub: DM Sans 300 · Handle/footer: DM Sans 300, low-opacity clear
- **Removed in v5 — never use:** Playfair Display, Bodoni Moda, Libre Baskerville

**Symbol { • }:** brackets framing a gold dot. Top-left on social covers. Functions: status indicator, numbering device {1}{2}{3}, logo/watermark. Never decorative in body copy.

**Photography:** faceless working moments only — laptop detail, coffee + laptop, document review, desk material detail. Desaturated, warm-dark (sepia-adjacent, never cold blue), visible grain, available light, intentional imperfection. Authority signals (spreadsheets, annotated documents, multi-device setups) — never lifestyle signals (ceramic mugs styled, linen, candles). Velum must never look like it belongs in the same visual category as the clients it serves.

## Shadow model — public language

Never name it. Never describe it. Hint only:
- "Some engagements go further — by invitation only."
- "For a small number of Blueprint clients, the work doesn't stop there."
- "Entry point. Some partnerships go deeper."
